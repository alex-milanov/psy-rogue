# Game Inspirations

Core influences and mechanics borrowed from various games.

## Primary Inspirations

### Deus Ex (2000)
- **Augmentation system** - Upgradeable abilities
- **Multiple approaches** - Stealth, combat, social
- **Hub areas** - Safe zones for NPCs, missions, shops
- **Conspiracy narrative** - Corporate intrigue
- **Choice and consequence**

### Oni (2001)
- **Hand-to-hand combat** - Combo system
- **Cyberpunk Japanese aesthetic**
- **Female protagonist** (potential)
- **Fluid movement system**

### Syndicate (1993)
- **Isometric tactical view** - Top-down commanding
- **Persuadertron mechanic** - Convert enemies to your side (psychic theme!)
- **Team of agents** - Squad management
- **Dark cyberpunk atmosphere** - Rain, neon, corporate coldness
- **Mission briefings** - Top-down map overview
- **Research/upgrade system**

### GTA Series
- **GTA 3**: Camera switching (3rd person ↔ top-down)
- **GTA Series**: Open world hub areas, safe houses, shops
- **Urban environment** - Streets, NPCs, vehicles
- **Mission structure** - Accept missions from NPCs

### Vampire: The Masquerade - Redemption (2000)
- **Camera interaction** - Seamless switching between 3D and top-down views
- **Gothic/dark atmosphere**
- **Hub and dungeon structure** - Safe areas + combat zones

### Diablo I & II
- **Town (static) + Dungeon (procedural)** structure
- **Procedurally generated levels** - Replayability
- **Loot and progression**
- **Safe hub area** - NPCs, shops, quests

## Camera System (Implemented)

✅ **Current**: Seamless 3D ↔ top-down switching via mouse drag
- Inspired by: GTA 3, Vampire: The Masquerade - Redemption
- Works smoothly without mode buttons
- Natural feel for tactical overview
